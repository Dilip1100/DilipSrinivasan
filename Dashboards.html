Casestudy TABLEAU

Cyclistic, a bike-share company in Chicago, wants to maximize the number of annual memberships by understanding how casual riders and annual members use Cyclistic bikes differently. This analysis was carried out using a public data set, and the results help make strategic business decisions using quarterly generated data files. Visualization of the findings was created to answer the question “In what ways do members and casual riders use Divvy bikes differently?”
